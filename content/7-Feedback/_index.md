### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is professional, friendly, and open. FCAJ members are always ready to support when I encounter difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace is arranged neatly, creating favorable conditions to focus and improve work efficiency. Besides, if there were more regular exchange activities, the bonding between members would be further enhanced.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
Throughout the internship, the mentor always closely followed, dedicatedly guided, and explained in detail the professional content when needed. Instead of just providing solutions, the mentor created conditions for me to proactively learn, experiment, and solve problems on my own, thereby improving my thinking and independence in work. In addition, the Admin Team also effectively supported procedures, documentation, and issues related to the internship, helping me quickly integrate into the working environment.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
Although the tasks and practices were slightly different from my major, those practical exercises brought me a lot of new knowledge, which is somewhat related to my personal development process in the future. This knowledge gave me the opportunity to better understand the real-world environment, update myself on new market trends, and open up a new perspective on career opportunities that school does not offer.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I learned many new skills such as using project management tools, teamwork skills, software management processes, and professional communication in a corporate environment. The insights shared by the mentor and the admins also helped me gain a more practical perspective and a clearer direction for my career development process.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is very positive: everyone respects each other, values cooperation, and supports one another. Team members always coordinate effectively to achieve common goals, especially during periods with heavy workloads. This helped me quickly integrate into the team, confidently discuss work, and gain more motivation to learn throughout the internship.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company provides internship allowance and offers flexible working hours when necessary. In addition, participating in internal training sessions, sharing professional knowledge, and skill development activities brought many practical values, helping me accumulate more experience and prepare better for my job after graduation.


- **What I found most satisfying during my internship:**  
  What I found most satisfying was the professional, friendly working environment that always encourages a learning spirit. The mentor and team members were always ready to support, creating conditions for me to proactively research, experiment, and solve problems. Besides, I was able to participate directly in project tasks, thereby accumulating a lot of practical knowledge and experience.

- **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?:**  
  I am willing to recommend the internship program to friends because this is a suitable environment to learn and develop. Interns are not only guided dedicatedly but also given opportunities to participate in actual tasks, thereby improving professional knowledge, working skills, and accumulating experience in a corporate environment.
